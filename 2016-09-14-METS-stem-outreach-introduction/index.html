Tragedy of the commons.
The game we saw there is called the Prisoner's Dilemma. Mathematicians can expand it to study many players and then it's called the Tragedy of the Commons. It can be used to show how when we all act rationally we can overuse a limited resource. This is in part what is causing the environmental problems we are faced with today.
